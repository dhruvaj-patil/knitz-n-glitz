What is Knitz N Glitz?

Knitz N Glitz is a premier fashion event dedicated to uncovering and promoting the finest designers and models from Seven Sisters and One Brother. We’re on a mission to showcase the region’s most dazzling designers and striking models, propelling them onto both national and international platforms. If you’ve got the talent, we’ve got the spotlight!

Our Goal

Our mission? Simple. We aim to handpick the best designers and models from the Northeast and equip them with the tools and platform to shine globally. Our goal is to catapult Northeast India’s fashion scene onto the world stage.

FAQs
1.
Who can participate?
Whether you’re a kid, a model of any gender, rocking plus-size, or senior citizen, if you’ve got talent, we’ve got a stage just for you.
2.
Where is the event happening?
Right at the heart of Guwahati, Assam, in the dazzling Roodraksh Mall.
3.
For how long the contest will run?
This one-of-a-kind event will span over 36 days with multiple rounds of excitement.
4.
Can I participate if I am not from Guwahati?
Absolutely! This event is a Northeast-wide celebration, so if you’re from any corner of the seven sisters and one brother, then you’re more than welcome to join the fashion extravaganza.
