FAQs

Who can participate?
Whether you’re a kid, a model of any gender, rocking plus-size, or senior citizen, if you’ve got talent, we’ve got a stage just for you.
2.
Where is the event happening?
Right at the heart of Guwahati, Assam, in the dazzling Roodraksh Mall.

This one-of-a-kind event will span over 36 days with multiple rounds of excitement.
